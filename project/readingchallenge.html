Reading challenge 2023

My goal is to read at least 15 books 2023. I have done similar goals previous years, but never really achieved what I wanted. With 2022 looking to be 6-7 books, out of my goal of 12 you can easily say i have underachieved. By having my challenge public like this, perhaps it will incentivize me to try harder - to cut the slack.

Reading 15 books a year is approximately reading one book every 3.5 weeks. This is achievable. And considering I read 12 back in 2019, I can read 3 more 4 years later. The downhill trend as you can see to the right simply has to stop. 4 books in 2021!? One every 3 months... Shameful.

In addition to having this reading challenge I will try to review the books I read, writing about what I think of the book and what not.
