Reading plan 2023

My goal is to read 15 books 2023, which averages around one book every 3.5 weeks. A bit more than one book a month. Here's my list of books I plan to read. It's not in any particular order, but if it is listed, I aim to read it.

  • Postcapitalist Deisre: The Final Lectures, by Mark Fisher
  • The Perennial Philosophy, by Aldous Huxley
  • Room to Dream, by David Lynch
  • Once Upon A Time In Hollywood, by Quentin Tarantino
  • Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones, by James Clear
  • The Sailor Who Fell from Grace with Sea, by Yokio Mishima
  • Meditations, by Marcus Aurelius
  • Building and Dwelling: Ethics for the City, by Richard Sennett
  • A Confederacy of Dunces, by John Kennedy Toole
  • Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas, by Hunter S. Thompson
  • The Courage of Hopelessness, by Slavoj Zizek
  • Thus Spake Zarathustra, by Friedrich Nietzsche
  • What I Talk About When I Talk About Running, by Haruki Murakami
  • The Metamorphosis, by Franz Kafka
  • The Castle, by Franz Kafka
